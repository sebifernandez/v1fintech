Q & A / Cardholder

1

How does my V1 card work?

Your V1 card works in the same way as a payment card but without an associated bank account. You can never spend more than the available on the card. It can be loaded in US dollars (USD) but you can spend in any currency in the world where MasterCard is accepted.

2

How do I access the available and list of transactions of my V1 account?

You can download the V1 App, for to check your balance and transactions in the most comfortable way. You can also access on our website: www.v1fintech.com, login to your account and navigate throughout cards and transactions.

3

How safe is may V1 payment card?

It is the safest way. As soon as your card is activated, your expense budget will be limited to your available balance. As an extra safety control, with just a click, V1 admin user will be able to temporarily deactivate or definitely terminate cards anytime.

4

What happens if I loose a card?

V1 Admin user can immediately terminate any lost card, you can also report your V1 payment card by calling V1 Customer Service.

5

What do I do with the remaining balance in my V1 payment card after each flight?

V1 grants full ownership of budget allocation to their admin users. Thus, they will have full flexibility to withdraw any remaining balance, leave it stand by card for a next flight, deactivate until next flight or even terminate and issue a new card.

6

As a V1 cardholder, what should I check before traveling?

1. Make sure your card is active.
2. Check your balance using your Mobile App, call, email or chat with V1 customer service desk (see contact info bellow).
3. Remember the emergency number to report the theft or loss of your V1: 1-866.410.3181.

7

How to load money to V1 Mastercard cards?

Your Aviation company admin user is able to reload your V1 card on real time based on available funds.

8

How long does it take to get the money loaded on the V1 card?

Funds will be available on the spot for sufficient fund accounts, 24/7/365.

Q & A / Admin User

1

How does V1 card work?

Your V1 card works in the same way as a payment card but without an associated bank account. Expending limit is subject to available balance on the card. It can be loaded in US dollars (USD) but you can spend in any currency in the world where MasterCard is accepted.

2

How do we manage our V1 Cards?

Login to your Card Admin user account by visiting www.v1fintech.com. Fund master account, issue, activate, fund, terminate cards anytime, anywhere.

3

How do we manage V1 card transactions?

Login to your Expenses Management user account by visiting www.v1fintech.com. Review transactions per contact, card number, date range, create users, populate and export reports for bank reconciliation.

4

Are there any costs and Fees for V1 Card transactions?

V1 has no monthly or maintenance costs, transaction or cancelation fees.

5

Is there a minimum amount required to fund the card?

There is a $1 minimun amount for the initial loading of the V1 card or for subsequent top-ups. There is no limit to the number of recharges by card.

6

How long does it take to get the money loaded on the V1 card?

Funds will be available on the spot for sufficient fund accounts, 24/7/365.

7

What happens if a card gets lost?

As the Admin Users, you will be able to immediately terminate any lost card, you can also report your V1 payment card by calling V1 Customer Service 24/7/365.
